## Description

**Resume Studio turns a single job description into your entire application package — resume, cover letter, LinkedIn summary, cold email, interview prep, certification guide, and follow-up email — in one click.**

Paste a job description, pick your documents, and get a complete, tailored application package generated by AI. Every document is optimized for the specific role, not generic filler.

**What makes it different:**

- **Roast My Resume** — Get a brutally honest AI teardown of your resume. Free, no signup, no email. It's the viral entry point that actually helps people.
- **Real-time ATS scoring** — See how your resume scores against the job description before you hit apply. Stop guessing if you'll get past the robots.
- **13 PDF templates** — 3 free, 10 premium. From corporate to creative, every template is designed to pass ATS parsers while looking sharp to human reviewers.
- **11 languages** — Generate your documents in English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Hindi, Arabic, and Italian.
- **Career Coach AI** — An ongoing conversation with an AI career advisor that remembers your context and gives specific, actionable guidance.
- **Job feed + tracker** — Search jobs and track your applications in one place. No more spreadsheet chaos.

**The free tier is real.** 2 AI generations per month, 3 templates, ATS scoring, PDF downloads. No credit card required, no trial that expires.

Paid plans start at $5.99/mo for more generations and all templates. Pro at $10.99/mo unlocks multi-language, premium fonts, and Career Coach.

We built this because job applications are broken. You shouldn't spend 45 minutes customizing a resume for every role. You should spend 45 seconds.

## First Comment / Maker Story

Hey Product Hunt!

I built Resume Studio because I was tired of the resume grind. Every job application felt like starting from scratch — rewrite the resume, draft a cover letter, tailor the LinkedIn summary, compose the cold email. Multiply that by 20+ applications and you've lost a week of your life to formatting and rewording.

So I built a tool that does all of it from a single job description. Paste the JD, pick what you need, click generate. You get a tailored resume, cover letter, LinkedIn summary, cold email, interview prep guide, certification roadmap, and follow-up email. All optimized for that specific role.

The feature I'm most proud of is **Roast My Resume**. It's completely free — no signup, no email capture, nothing. Just upload your resume and get an honest, slightly savage AI breakdown of what's working and what isn't. I made it free because I think everyone deserves to know if their resume is holding them back, regardless of whether they pay for anything.

The stack is Next.js + Supabase + Claude, hosted on Vercel. Every document goes through real ATS scoring so you know exactly where you stand before you apply.

I'd love your feedback. Try the roast — it's free and takes 30 seconds.

## Screenshot Suggestions

Capture these pages/states for the Product Hunt gallery (recommended order):

1. **Hero / Landing page** — Shows the value prop and "Start Free" CTA. First impression.
2. **Roast My Resume results** — A completed roast showing the score breakdown, category grades, and the "brutal honesty" tone. This is the viral hook.
3. **Generate page with all 7 documents selected** — The one-click flow: JD pasted, all document types checked, ready to generate.
4. **Generated documents view** — Multiple completed documents fanned out or in a list, showing the breadth of output from a single generation.
5. **ATS Score results** — A resume scored against a JD, showing the match percentage and specific improvement suggestions.
6. **Template gallery** — Grid view of all 13 templates, showing visual variety.
7. **Career Coach conversation** — An ongoing AI coaching chat showing personalized career advice.
8. **Pricing page** — Clean tier comparison showing the free tier prominently.

**Tips:**
- Use a real (anonymized) resume for screenshots, not lorem ipsum
- Show the dark UI — it photographs well and stands out in the PH gallery
- Capture at 2x resolution (Retina) for crisp thumbnails
- Lead with the Roast or the 7-document generation — those are the "wait, what?" moments
